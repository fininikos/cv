ABOUT

I am a Business Performance Management Consultant with a designer’s mindset and a deep belief in the power of empathy. I find joy in listening closely to leaders and teams, understanding their challenges, and translating their vision into strategies that are not only innovative but also practical and human-centered.

My work combines strategic thinking with hands-on execution. By leveraging methodologies such as Design Thinking, Lean Six Sigma, and the OKRs framework, I guide businesses in defining their vision, structuring their organisations, and designing roadmaps that connect ambition with measurable outcomes. I also help define KPIs, shape organisational cultures, and craft brand-led strategies that resonate emotionally while delivering commercial impact.

Having collaborated directly with executives, I have learned that the real value lies not only in creating a strong plan but in staying present throughout execution - monitoring progress, ensuring alignment, and adapting strategies as businesses grow. I see my role as both a strategic partner and a trusted guide, helping organisations achieve sustainable success while cultivating cultures of purpose, accountability, and growth.

Ultimately, my personal aim is to showcase the impact of empathy and good design in business: building structures that are resilient, brands that inspire, and strategies that truly come to life.
